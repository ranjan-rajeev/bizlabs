FACETS OF INNOVATION @ABG

At ABG we believe innovation is not just about products, rather it has many facets. We are looking for innovation in SALES (e.g. channel partners as owners), PROCESS (e.g. Open innovation process) ORGANIZATIONAL (e.g. Marketing  Marketing & Innovation) PRODUCTION (e.g. IoT to improve Energy efficiency) VALUE CHAIN (e.g. Accredited partners) CONSUMER SOLUTION’s (e.g. Cement  Home solns) COMMERCIAL (e.g. Cost re-engineering) and BUSINESS MODELS (e.g. Value creation: Scale-up Equity)
